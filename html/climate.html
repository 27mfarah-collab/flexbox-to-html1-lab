Antartica

Antartica is losing ice mass because the ice caps are melting. The local penguin population has been declining slowly. Climate temperature is shifting towards warmer weather which disrupts the natural ecosystem.

The animals and ecosytem will be heavily reduced. The sea level will rise exponetionally. The southocean will lose it's ability to absorb carbon dioxide and regulate heat.

Impacts of climate Change in antartica

"Antarctica is showing worrying signs of rapid change across its ice, ocean and ecosystems," lead author and Australian National University professor Nerilie Abram told the Agence France-Presse. "Some of these abrupt changes will be difficult to stop."
- Prof. Nerilie Abram
